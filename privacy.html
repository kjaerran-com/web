Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how As-the-crow-flies ("we", "us", or "the site") collects, uses, and discloses information when you visit our website. By using the site you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We do not require you to register, create an account, or submit personal information to use the site's core features (searching cities, comparing countries, viewing the globe). We collect the following limited information automatically:

  • Server logs and analytics: standard web server data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time of visit.
  • Search queries: when you search for a city, the search term you type is sent to a third-party geocoding API (Open-Meteo) so we can return matching results. We do not store these queries on our servers.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section 3 below.

2. How we use information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the site.
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and security issues.
  • Display advertisements (see Section 4).
  • Aggregate anonymous usage statistics.

3. Cookies

We and our third-party providers use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, measure performance, and serve relevant advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; doing so may affect site functionality.

4. Advertising and Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display ads. Google may use cookies (including the "DART cookie") and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can review Google's advertising practices and opt out of personalised advertising at:

  • Google's Advertising Policies
  • Google Ads Settings (control how Google personalises ads for you)
  • aboutads.info (industry opt-out tool)
  • Your Online Choices (EU opt-out tool)

Third-party advertisers and ad networks served through AdSense may also use their own cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies. We do not have access to or control over these technologies. Their use is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Third-party services

The site loads data and assets from the following third-party services. Each may collect technical information (such as your IP address) when their resources load in your browser:

  • Open-Meteo Geocoding API — used to search for cities worldwide.
  • Natural Earth via GitHub / unpkg / cdnjs — used to load country outlines and 3D map textures.
  • Google AdSense — used to display advertising.

6. Data retention

We do not store personal information on our servers beyond the duration of your visit. Server logs may be retained by our hosting provider (Netlify) according to their own retention policies.

7. Children's privacy

The site is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under the EU/UK GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal information, and to opt out of personalised advertising. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details on our Contact page.

9. International transfers

Because the site is delivered over the global internet, your information may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where data protection laws may differ.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent through our Contact page.
